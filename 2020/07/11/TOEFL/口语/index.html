独立口语

结构

开门见山亮主题，1-2个理由，要对原因做展开

思路

  • 一个中心，两种展开方式

中心:添加更多的细节，加入具体事物
两种展开方式:

  • 说理(讲道理)：
    1. 原因展开
      1. 主题句
      2. because，原因句，话题展开
      3. Also/Besides，原因句，话题展开
      4. (So, that’s why I think 主题句)
    2. 找不同的受益方受损方
      1. 主题句
      2. First, it is (not) good for A
      3. Second, it is (not) good for B
  • 举例(讲故事)：
    1. 举自己的例子
    2. 造人设
      关键：要有具体细节，可适当加入背景引入

主要思路

花钱、花时间、放松解压、增长见识、室友、森林公园

模板

放松减压理由（适用于娱乐休闲类话题）

  • XXX can help me to ease my mind .
  • You know ,nowadays ,students’ lives
    are very stressful . Everyday I have a lot
    of assignments and exams ,which drive
    me crazy .
  • So if /every time I XXX , it can make me
    relax and unwind ,(可加入细节或略过)，
    and give me a relaxing moment

增长见识

  • Because it can help me broaden my horizons
    / open my mind , I can acquire a lot of
    knowledge from it .（可加细节）
  • these things can largely enrich my knowledge
    structure , and make me more
    knowledgeable and insightful ,and finally
    help me see things in a wider perspective

    例子

    Is it a good idea that students should be
    allowed to keep pets in their dorms ?

I don’t think it is a good idea for students to keep pets in dorms.
Because keeping a pet usually costs students some extra
money. You know, in order to provide their pets like dogs or
kitties with a good environment, students have to spend some
money purchasing toys with them, affording food for them,
which will increase their financial burden.
Also, keeping pets usually takes up so much time for students.
They have to spend quite a lot of time playing with their pets
and feeding them, which prevents students from focusing on
their study.
