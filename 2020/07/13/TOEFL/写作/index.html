语言、思路、段落展开

独立写作

语言

状语做插入语

First, today’s television programs are full of action and suspense in order to attract viewers and increase ratings.

First, today’s television programs, in order to attract viewers and increase ratings, are full of action and suspense.

倒装

  • not only… but also…

Not only advertisements increase sales of the products, but they also provide consumers with plenty of valuable information.

  • seldom/hardly/never/under no circumstance
    Never should scientists use their brainpowers to engage in illegal or immoral activities.

强调

  • It be … that …

My friend could complete his college education because of this part-time job.

It was because of this part-time job that my friend could complete his college education.

What/all/only引导的主语从句

What children can learn from parents are important principles and values.

题型分类

  • 单一事物-价值判断
  • 单一事物-现象证明
  • 对比选择
  • 绝对词
  • 复合题干(因果/目的/条件)

单一事物-价值判断

  • 题目标志：should, it is important/ necessary/ good(表语)

  • 解题策略：进行利弊分析

Students should study abroad after they graduate.

agree：

  1. Enrich knowledge and widen horizons.

  2. Expand cultural diversity, improve communication skills.

  3. Foster a sense of independence.

disagree：

  1. Weaken fitness level due to the enormous stress of studying.

  2. Endanger safety owing to high crime rate. robbery, burglary etc.

  3. Lead to financial burden.

单一事物-现象证明

  • 题目标志：“是不是”/“会不会”/“能不能”
  • 解题策略：
    1. 分析现象存在的原因
    2. 拆分举例(更通用)

More and more students are choosing to study abroad after they graduate.

分析原因：

  1. Rising disposable income.
  2. Fierce competition in domestic country.
  3. Ideal academic atmosphere.

拆分举例：

  1. Consider “computer science” major students.(cutting-edge tech, high-salary jobs)

  2. Consider “animation & visual effects ” major students.(Mature film industry, Hollywood, Pixar Animation)

  3. Consider “international finance” major students.(Stock market, Wall street, NYSE, Nasdaq)

对比选择

  • 题目标志：题干中明确出现多个比较对象

  • 解题策略： 论证自己观点时，顾忌反方观点

It is more important for students to study abroad than to study domestically after they graduate.

  1. Heighten knowledge level and widen horizon.On the other hand,…

  2. Expand cultural diversity, improve communication skills.In contrast,…

  3. Foster a sense of independence.However, …

People benefit more from travelling in their own country than travelling in a foreign country.

Disagree：

  1. Obtain more information about nature and history (exotic landscapes and customs).

However, people familiarize with their own country, thereby making it unlikely to acquire new knowledge.
2. Improve communication skills by meeting friends worldwide.
But people in one’s own country share common cultural background.
3. Foster a sense of independence
whereas familiar surroundings restrains the exercise of one’s independent skill.

常考题型-政府投资型(A-B选项) ：

不比较重要性

  1. A让大部分居民受益，而B收益的人较小
  2. A所需资金巨大
  3. 不得不承认，B也需要资金，但数额小。
    且可通过其他方式来解决

Government should spend more money on improving public transportation than on Internet access.

  1. Public transportation benefits the majority of citizens, whereas internet access only benefits a few people.

  2. Public transportation costs more compared to internet access.

  3. Admittedly, internet access does request funding. However, it was a small amount. More importantly, it can be supported by commercial companies.

Government should spend more money on elementary education than on college education.

  1. Elementary education benefits the majority of citizens, whereas college education is elite education.

  2. Elementary education costs more compared to college education.

  3. Admittedly, college education does require budget. However, it was a small amount. Moreover, it can be supported by commercial companies or alumni donation.

常考题型-今昔对比

  1. 科技水平
  2. 人们的观念/意识(主观)
  3. 社会趋势(客观)

People rely on their neighbors less now than they did in the past.

  1. Nowadays, people can rely on internet to solve problems rather neighbors such as Google and Craigslist.
    However, in the past,…

  2. It is hard to trust people especially neighbors compared to the past.

  3. People are living fast-paced lives so that they can hardly chitchat. In contrast, people in the past seldom work overtime.

Nowadays it is easier to be educated than in the past.

  1. Nowadays, modern technologies enable people to study anytime, anywhere.
    MOOC (massive open online courses) such as Coursera becomes prevalent.
    However, books in the past…

  2. People start to realize the significance of education, while in the past…

  3. More schools in academic and vocational fields are emerging, exposing people to more education chances.
    By comparison, in the past…

常考题型-三选一(A,B,C)

  1. 可选出胜出者(更好)
    1. B-
    2. C-
    3. A+
      找最难成为反面的点为A
  2. 三个都不错，看谁优点多 (更通用)
    1. A+1
    2. A+2
    3. Admittedly， B+/C+. However,…

常见跑题：

  1. 都好
  2. 只写自己选的点

Which way do you think is the best for a student to make new friends? 1)joining a sports team 2) participate in community activities 3) traveling

  1. There are many conflicts and body collisions.
  2. It is hard to maintain the connection with friends met in traveling and they are ended up with drifting apart.
  3. Communication and collaboration in community activities can deepen the relationship and cultivate mutual trust.

What do you think contributes the most to an enjoyable vacation: good food, good location or traveling with good friends?

  1. Prevalence of different foreign cuisine enables people to eat favorable food in local restaurants.
  2. Adjust to friends’ preference and habits etc.
  3. Landspace, Culture

绝对词

  • 题目标志： never, always, the most, the best
  • 解题策略：
    1. 承认合理性
    2. 反面影响
    3. 其他可能性
  • 观点句
    1. Generally speaking, it is true that…
    2. However, to say it is 绝对词 is an overstatement or exxaggreration.

Advertising is the most important cause of unhealthy eating habits.

  1. Generally speaking, it is true that advertising can enable people to obtain unhealthy eating habbits. However to say it is the most important cause is an overstatement.
  2. Junk food ads use exaggerating cases and celebrities’ endorsement to mislead people to eat unhealthy food.(McDonald’s Coca-Cola)
  3. Some ads, on the contrary, will help people foster positive eating habits
  4. Fast-paced modern life, work overtime, junk food cost much less, food culture

复合题干

  • 题目标志：
    1. 因果关系 (因为A，所以B)
    2. 目的/条件关系 If/To A, B
  • 解题策略：
    1. 因果关系（不同意）：
      • 反驳原因（X A）
      • 另寻他果（A->C）
      • 另寻他因（E->B）
    2. 目的/条件（同意）：
      • 展开B在A限制条件下讨论即可

Students should study abroad after they graduate because they could receive better education.
Disagree:

  1. Studying abroad does not guarantee a better education.
    • Undeveloped countries.
    • Without parents’ supervision, go astray(误入歧途).
  2. Despite a better education, it does not follow students study abroad. Other options, take online course such as MOOC, coursera.
  3. Some other reasons may lead to studying abroad.
    • become more competitive in job hunting
    • Enlarge friends’ circle

It is not a good idea to move to a new place because of the loss of old friends.

Disagree：

  1. It is not a good idea to move to a new place because of the loss of old friends.
  • keep in touch via WhatsApp or Skype.
  1. We should not give up valuable opportunities such as studying abroad or relocating workplace just because of losing old friends.
  • Pursuing one’s own path is more crucial.
  1. Maybe the reason why people should not move to a new place is due to weather/food/family connection.
  • Maybe the reason why people should not move to a new place is due to weather/food/family connection.

For the purpose of achieving more advanced knowledge, students should study abroad after they graduate.
Agree:

  1. Prestigious professors can convey more advanced knowledge. Some Ivy league Colleges, Nobel prize owner may teach directly

  2. More advanced facilities can guarantee more advanced knowledge. Biology, DNA, experiment, more accurate

  3. Classmates around the world may spark new ideas to solve academic problems.

让步段

常规段落结构 带有让步段的段落结构
SP1 SP1
SP2 SP2
SP3 让步段

故意写不利于自己观点的内容，最终对其进行削弱和反驳

写法

Admittedly (Granted), 反面论证. For instance, 适度展开. However, 削弱/反驳

削弱和反驳的方法

  1. 变成次要内容
    句型：

  • On balance, 正面内容 far outweigh 反面内容.

  • Compared with 正面内容, 反面内容 seems to be minor/ secondary.

  1. 提出解决方案
    句型：
    As long as 解决措施 is proposed, the problem can be resolved. Hence, there is nothing to worry about.

例子

  • 单一事物

    • A+1
    • A+2
    • 让步，Admittedly,A-,For instance…However,削弱反驳(解决措施/次要内容)

  • 对比选择

    • A+1/B-1
    • A+2/B-2
    • 让步，Admittedly, A-/B+, For instance… However,削弱反驳(若是A-，则可以解决措施/次要内容；若B+，则只能用次要内容)

It is more fun to see a movie in the cinema with other people than see a movie at home.

Admittedly, there are multiple benefits when watching at home. We can watch the movie in our own way such as lying on the sofa, and screaming loudly. In addition, we can pause while you have something else to deal with such as picking up the phone. However, compared with the strong visual impact and excellent enjoyable watching atmosphere, the merit brought by watching at home seems to be secondary.

Do you agree or disagree with the following statement? It is better for parents to let children manage their pocket money when they were young.

Admittedly, some children may go astray when managing their pocket money such as addicting to alcohol and smoking. However, under the supervision and guidance from parents like illustrating the severe consequences, such negative situations can be avoided.

Introduction

  1. Background 背景
    • 选择题干中的一个关键词，从如下三个方面展开。做到背景部分与题目话题相关，但没有完全对应。
    • Importanca:
      • A plays an important (essential/ vital/ significant) role in B.
      • A is an integral part of B
      • A places a premium on B.
      • Much emphasis is placed on B.
        • Influence:
      • A has a widespread/ considerable/significant/huge/ tremendous/enormous/positive influence/ effect/ impact on B.
        • Prevalence:
        • There is a general/growing trend that ….
        • It is a common practice that….

  1. Controversy 争议

    • However, people have conflicting/differing opinions about whether…
    • However, the opinions about ……are varied.
    • …… is a controversial topic.
    • …… is under fierce discussion.
    • …… remains a heated topic.
    • 适当改写：同义词替换/句式改变/具体指代抽象

  2. Main point 观点

    • I believe/ In my opinion/ As far as I am concerned/ From my perspective/ I stick to the idea that

A/D：People would be happier with fewer possessions.

关键词： happy， possessions

Nowadays society places a premium on one’s personal properties. People have differing opinions about whether more possessions will degrade one’s happiness index. As far as I am concerned, I totally disagree that people will acquire more happiness with fewer possessions.

A/D：To achieve successful development of a country, a government should focus its budgets more on young children’s education rather than on universities.

Education had tremendous influence on both children’s psychological development and college students’ future careers. Which education stage, early childhood education or elite’s education deserves more funding is under heated debate. From my perspective, I stick to the idea that the cognitive development matters most when kids are little, thereby deserving more budgets.

Technology has made children less creative than they were in the past.

Over recent years it is a common practice that technology has dramatically changed the way children live their lives in various aspects. Among them, people have conflicting opinions about whether technology has deteriorated the formation of children’s creativity compared with the past. I believe that technology restrains children from growing to creative individuals.

  • 分论点之间应该互斥并前置与段首
  • 关键： 段落的充分展开
  1. 说理
    • 因果
    • 对比
  2. 例证
    • 真实（人/物）
    • 假象（人/物）
    • 名人轶事
    • 名人名言
    • 调查研究数据报告

说理

因果关系

  • It is because/主句, because从句.

  • The reason (for n./why+句子) is that

  • A is due to/ owing to B.

  • A is attributed/ attributable/ ascribed to B.

  • B creates/ generates/ produces/ introduces A.

  • B leads to/results in A.

  • B is responsible for A.

First, watching television causes serious damage to children’s physical health. The reasons are threefold. First, if children spend an excessive amount of time on watching television, they will have less time for sports. Second, excessive television-viewing strains children’s eyes. Third, children tend to have large quantities of junk food while watching television. As a result, their physical health will be undermined.

对比关系

  • otherwise, yet, rather, conversely, nevertheless, nonetheless, whereas

  • in comparison, on the other hand, on the contrary, in contrast

Secondly, making school uniforms mandatory improves students’ academic performances. It is because uniforms help students to concentrate 100% on their studies. Otherwise they will be distracted by matters like “what do I wear to school?” “Which pair of shoes go with that dress.”

举例子

  • to substantiate, as an illustration, in fact

Newspapers and magazines are the best ways to learn about a foreign country.

  • 什么报纸，什么信息，什么方式

For example, I bought the 6th issue of “Traveler” depicting the travelling guide to San Ya in Hainan Province in China (Phuket/ Hawaii/ Maldives). It recommends hotels and resorts with best ocean scenery with a rank of service quality. Besides, it also introduces some of local, secret restaurants with best indigenous food and the driving direction.

  1. 假想例子：可能性的详细分类

Mobile phones have facilitated our lives in many different ways. For example, when a violent crime occurs, such as robbery, or burglary, we can dial 911 for help and report the crime. Besides, when we encounter mathematical or physical difficulties, we can use the App installed on the mobile phone to look up equation efficiently. Finally, relationships with friends and family members can be improved via frequently chatting with them in WhatsApp or Skype.

综合写作

  • 阅读：3分钟，不需要记笔记
  • 听力：2分钟左右，需要记笔记
  • 综合写作：20分钟，200字左右

阅读

  1. 阅读的观点是什么？猜测争论的要点是什么
  2. 总结每个分论段的内容
  • 阅读中观点和分论点都需要改写，不能照抄

听力

  1. 听力的第一段可以只听不写，无关键信息，从first开始记笔记
  2. 可以用自己的话来概括听力内容
  • 阅读分论点和听力分论点相对应
  • 听力细节：解释听力分论点正确的理由（reason），具体例子不是采分点
